Proposal for work


Location: 206 Lawn DR Kernersville, NC

Objective

Repair fallen ceiling in bedrooom. Repair popcorn texture. Patch holes in ceiling in hallway and paint ceiling. Repair crack in ceiling and match texture in 2nd bedroom. Repair and patch master bedroom closet. Install trim at attic access.Paint ceiling, walls, and trim in both bedrooms.

Prep

Step 1: (ceiling) Use a 2x4 brace to jack ceiling back flat and screw off once flush.

Step 2: Remove the texure at the big dips thgat is visible in the drywall behind the existing texture. Once removed, make mud passes untill the dips are no longer visible.

Patch and fill holes in hallway and mud until even. The 2nd bedroom, cut out the crack and remove the texture at area of rapair, and tape and mud.

Step 3: Once all ceilings are ready, and all walls are covered with plastic, re-blow texure and blend with existing.

Step 4:(paint) Repair and patch all holes and dameges in walls, especially in the master bedroom closet. Check for peeling trim and remove whats not bonding and prime. Once bedrooms are ready, paint ceilings, walls, and trim.

Final: At the end we will get up all materil, check for any drips/runs, clean up and dust down all work areas, etc. At that time a final walk through can be done.
